How CIS-Prox Works

Step 1: Network Validation

The system checks your IP address. Access is granted only if you're connected to University Wi-Fi. If you leave campus, you're automatically flagged as "Disconnected."

Step 2: Room Sign-In

Upon entering a room or a lab, log your presence via the web portal. This creates an active record linking you, the room, and the time.

Step 3: Peer Search

Find a classmate by entering their name. The system displays their current location.

Key Features

Geofenced Attendance

Students cannot cheat attendance from home. They must be on the school network to enter a room.

Live Peer Directory

A searchable list showing which students are currently in which rooms and labs.

Auto-Status Updates

If Wi-Fi connection is lost, your mapping status automatically updates to "Offline."
